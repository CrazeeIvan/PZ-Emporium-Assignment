About CrazeeIvan

Modder, developer, and Project Zomboid enthusiast

Who I Am

Welcome! I'm CrazeeIvan, a passionate Project Zomboid modder and developer with years of experience in creating content for the community. My journey with Project Zomboid began as a player who wanted to enhance the survival experience, and it evolved into a full-fledged modding career.

What started as simple tweaks and adjustments has grown into a collection of comprehensive mods and tools that thousands of players use daily. I believe in making modding accessible to everyone, which is why I focus on creating both player-facing mods and developer tools.

What I Do

I specialize in creating mods, tools, and utilities for the Project Zomboid community. My work focuses on three main areas:

  • Gameplay Mods: Enhancing survival mechanics, adding new features, and improving quality of life
  • Development Tools: Creating utilities that help other modders build better content
  • Custom Commissions: Working directly with players to create personalized mod experiences
